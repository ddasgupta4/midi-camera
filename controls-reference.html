Quick Tips

  • No left hand = diatonic default quality
  • Chords debounce at 350ms to avoid misfires
  • Left hand gestures are smoothed over 12 frames
  • Press Q to quit, ESC for config
  • Example: Right 4 fingers + Left curled = iv (minor four)
  • Example: Right open hand + Left pinch = V7 (dominant)
